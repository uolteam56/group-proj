Welcome to the Computer Science Theme Park! Hosting traditional amusement park rides for all the thrill lovers out there along with historical and educational attractions, there is something here for everyone! With such a variety of features, we are truly the first of our kind. Please take a tour through our extensive array of activities and make sure to book tickets in advance!

Fractal Land

Experience an ever-changing gallery

Fractals are complex patterns characterized by repeated simple processes that repeat an infinite number of times. Here, we harness this concept to provide a spectacle of constantly evolving art.

Immerse yourself in these works using our fractal mirror, which uses your own reflection to create a one of a kind art piece to treasure.

Book tickets now!

Museum and Meet the Stars!

Take a tour through the Museum of Computer Science History. This is home to many of the invaluable inventions that pioneered the development of computers and the way we use technology today.

As part of our tour, we invite guests to come meet the stars of technology- Spend time with some of the legendary machines from pop culture, including C-3PO, R2-D2, WALL-E, HAL 9000 and more!

COVID Safety

We are committed to upholding all COVID-19 safety guidlines for the protection of our guests. All staff are required to wear face masks and disposable gloves at all times. We invite our guests to do the same. A small bottle of hand sanitizer also is provided to all guests upon entry. Addiontally, there are hand sanitization stations set up at several areas around the park.
